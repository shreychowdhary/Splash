Info

Splash is a school-wide game of tag.  Your goal is to splash your target with water before another person who has your name splashes you first.  If you successfully splash someone, their target now becomes your new target.  The game lasts until there is only one person left and this person receives an award of $100. The person who splashes the most people will also receive a prize.

You will NOT be able to sign in if you have not signed up, but will be able to see the homepage.

1.  You must have your splash code on you at all times.  When you get splashed, you must show the splasher your code so that they can input it into the website to get their next target.  The splasher now attempts to pursue their next target.  

 

          ****If your target has lost their code you may email splashmaster2017@gmail.com to receive it.

 

2.  You may only splash someone with water (no sodas, juices, or etc.).  You can use whatever method you want (water bottles, water guns, water balloons, and more but nothing that will harm someone else)

 

3.  No splashing indoors or within 10 feet of a building's entrance.  

 

4.  No splashing your target if they are at a required school commitment (no sports practices/games, theatre/SDC rehearsals, or lectures).  However, you may splash them on their way to and from these events.  

 

5.  No stealing/destroying property in order to splash your target (aka no blackmailing or taking people’s things).

 

6. You cannot splash someone off campus (Princeton, New York City, home, etc.)

 

7. If you put in wrong codes you will be assumed to be guessing and have to wait
